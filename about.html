Margarita Genyte

I am a futuristic designer and architect who recently graduated from the University of East London. I was introduced to the wonders of architecture in my childhood through the stories of my grandfather who was an Architect and Engineer. Travelling and living in different countries in Europe has continued to fuel my passion for architecture. The global diversity of structures, modernist experimentation and technological innovation in recent builds has reinforced my desire to be a part of this field. Six years of studies have provided me with the tools to create and visualise my ideas and now I am eager to be a part of the architecture industry; a team which makes this world a better place to live in.
